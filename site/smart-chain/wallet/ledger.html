    Ledger

    SCOL and coins on ScolCoin Chain are supported by the Ledger Nano S hardware wallet. The following describes how to use Ledger Nano S to connect to TC

    Requirements

    • A Ledger Nano S hardware wallet with latest firmware.
    • Latest version of Ethereum app installed
    • An internet connection and an available USB port. Use an adapter for USB-C ports
    • U2F compatible browsers: Google Chrome, Opera, FireFox, Brave

    App Installation Instructions

    Tip

    The existing Ethereum App is compatible with ScolCoin Chain

    1) Plug in and unlock your Ledger device, open Ledger Live on your computer, then open the "Manager" panel.

    2) Within the "Manager" pane, type in "Ethereum" in the search field. Locate "Ethereum", then click on "Install". (Note: Do not use the "ScolCoin Chain" App)

    3) The Ethereum app will now install on your Ledger device.

    Enabling contract data for SRC20

    Tip

    Please ensure your enabled contract data before transfering SRC20 tokens

    If you want to send BEP-20 tokens, you need to activate contract data on your device. Otherwise, invalid status 6a80 is returned.

    Steps:

    • Connect and unlock your Ledger device.
    • Open the Ethereum application.
    • Press the right button to navigate to Settings. Then press both buttons to validate.
    • In the Contract data settings, press both buttons to allow contract data in transactions.
    • The device displays Allowed.

    Connect with MetaMask

    ScolCoin Chain is compatible with MetaMask. Users can use MetaMask as a bridge between Ledger Nano S and ScolCoin Chain to make transactions to the ScolCoin Chain testnet.

    1) Connect MetaMask to SCOL Testnet by following this guide to add a custom network

      • Symbol: SCOL
      • Block Explorer

    • Mainnet

      • RPC URLs
      • ChainID: 0x38 (56 in decimal)
      • Symbol: SCOL
      • Block Explorer
