    Staking with extension wallet

    How to delegate your SCOL with extension wallet

    • Install ScolCoin Chain extension wallet

    • Go to the staking page:

      • Mainnet: https://www.scolcoin.com/en/staking
      • Testnet: https://testnet-staking.scolcoin.com/en/staking

    Testnet example

    • Click on “Connect Wallet”

    • Unlock your wallet

    • Click on “delegate” and input the amount

    • Confirm your transaction in extension wallet

    Success

    • your delegation overview

    You can see the staking history here

    How to redelegate your SCOL with extension wallet

    By staking you SCOL, you participate in the selection process of ScolCoin Chain validators and earn rewards. Staking SCOL is critical for securing the network. Validators can self-bond, meaning they can delegate SCOL to themselves, and they can also receive delegations from any other SCOL holders.

    Redelegations between a unique delegator, source validator, and destination validator can only happen once every 7 days

    • Click on redelegate

    • Select a new validator and redegelate amount

    • Confirm your transaction in extension wallet

    Success

    • Refresh and see your delegation changes
