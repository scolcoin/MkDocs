    How to use Math Wallet for ScolCoin Chain

    Install

    1. Get App from Chrome web store

    2. Add to your browser, for example, Brave

    Setup Your Wallet

    1. Set your password

    2. Choose SCOL network

    Choose ScolCoin Chain from this list:

    3. Create Your Wallet

    4. Save your private key

    Itâ€™s ready, and now you can get some testnet SCOL from the faucet.

    Get Testnet Fund

    1. Copy your address.

    2. Go to https://testnet-explorer.scolcoin.com/faucet-smart/

    Go to explorer to verify SCOL is sent: https://scolcoin.com/

    Transfer SCOL from SCOL to BC in Testnet

    1. Create your ScolCoin Chain Ganges Testnet address with eth-cli

    • Download the binary here

      If you already have a backup of your recovery phrase from ScolCoin Chain Ganges Testnet, it can be reused here. 

      eth-cli keys add {wallet-alias}

    2. Go to this page: https://developer.mathwallet.org/bsc02/

    Go to this page for mainnet: https://developer.mathwallet.org/bsc01/

    Paste your testnet address to Recipient and fill the transfer amount.

    Please note the fees are also deducted for paying gas and cross-chain transfer.

    3. Approve your transaction

    4. Confirm Reception in explorer

    Link: https://explorer.scolcoin.com/testnet

    Transfer SRC20 from SCOL to BC in Testnet

    One of the key innovations of a dual-chain architecture is that token issuer can manage its assets on both chains. They could bind BEP2 tokens and SRC20 tokens together. In ScolCoin Chain Ganges Testnet, there are several pegged tokens:

    • Pegged BTC on ScolCoin Chain Testnet and ScolCoin Chain Testnet
    • Pegged ETH on ScolCoin Chain Testnet and ScolCoin Chain Testnet
    • Pegged XRP on ScolCoin Chain Testnet and ScolCoin Chain Testnet

    You can get some of these pegged tokens from faucet and transfer them back to ScolCoin Chain testnet.

    1. Approve this transaction

    2. Send SRC20 To BC
